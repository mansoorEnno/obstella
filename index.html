The Product

Every department.
Thoughtfully engineered.

Not mockups — these are real screens from Obstella v1.0.1.

Financial Reporting

Know your numbers. Instantly.

Income by day, week, month, quarter or year — grand totals, per-category breakdowns and doctor-wise views, computed in under half a second even across years of records.

  • Pre-aggregated reporting engine — no waiting, ever
  • Group by date, category or doctor
  • One-click export to PDF or CSV
Bed Management

Your hospital, at a glance.

Every block, floor and room as live tiles — who's in which bed, which rooms are free, VIP availability — updated the moment an admission or discharge happens.

  • Rooms auto-book on admission, auto-release on discharge
  • Patient name and procedure on every occupied room
  • VIP rooms tracked separately
Collections

No rupee left behind.

Every unpaid balance in one prioritised worklist — oldest first, with the patient's mobile number right there for the follow-up call. Click through to record the payment.

  • Days-since-discharge aging on every row
  • Total outstanding always visible
  • One click from due to patient record
Patient Records

Every patient. One timeline.

Register once — every visit, surgery, payment and due builds a complete history. Search by name, guardian or mobile; filter by visit type and date; export to CSV.

  • Full visit and admission history per patient
  • Live admitted count in the header
  • Open any record in one click
Surgery & Billing

Admission to receipt, one flow.

Book the procedure, assign a pre-priced package and room, then discharge with the final bill, discount and balance computed instantly — and an A5 receipt on the printer.

  • Final bill = package cost − discount, automatic
  • Cash, card or bank payment modes
  • Length of stay tracked automatically
Front Desk

A visit in seconds.

One unified form for OPD, Private OPD, Lab and Ultrasound. Pick the patient, tap a visit-type chip, assign the doctor — fees auto-fill and lab tests aggregate themselves.

  • Multi-select test checklist with automatic totals
  • Queue token printed at registration
  • Backdate entries when catching up paperwork

Your Rules

🖨️

Tokens & Receipts

Tech Specs

Engineered to be invisible.

No servers. No cloud bills. No IT department. Obstella installs in minutes and runs for years.

Platform
Windows desktop applicationWindows 7 SP1 through Windows 11 · installer included
Architecture
3-layer .NET (Framework 4.8)Services · data repositories · WinForms UI — engineered for reliability
Database
Embedded SQLite, WAL modeSingle-file database — zero administration, years of data in one file
Performance
Pre-aggregated reporting engineIncome summaries maintained by database triggers — daily report <500 ms, yearly <3 s
Privacy
100% offline & on-premisePatient data never leaves the hospital PC
Security
Authenticated accessAccount lockout after 5 failed attempts · security-question / recovery-key reset
Backup
Automatic, multi-destinationMonthly auto-backup to up to 3 locations · integrity-verified · 30-copy retention
Printing
A5 receipts & queue tokensDischarge receipts, visit tokens with per-category daily numbering, PDF reports
System requirements
Any PC from the last 15 years4 GB RAM is plenty · launches in under 2 seconds
