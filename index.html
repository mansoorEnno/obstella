Features

Every department.
Thoughtfully engineered.

Twelve integrated modules, each designed around how a real hospital actually works.

📊

Executive Dashboard

Walk in every morning and know exactly how the hospital is doing. Live KPI tiles, income trend charts and a real-time admitted-patients board — switchable across daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly views.

  • OPD, Lab, Ultrasound & Surgery counts at a glance
  • Income and outstanding dues totals for any period
  • Currently admitted patients with room assignments
  • One-click PDF report for management
👤

Patient 360°

Register a patient once. Every visit, surgery, payment and due appears on a single timeline — with searchable directory, filters and CSV export.

Registration · Directory · History
🩺

Smart Visit Entry

One unified form for OPD, Private OPD, Lab and Ultrasound. Pick the patient, tap a visit-type chip, assign the doctor — fees auto-fill and totals calculate themselves.

5 visit types · Auto fees · Backdating
⚕️

Surgery, Start to Finish

Book the procedure, pick a pre-priced package, assign a room — Block → Floor → Room. The room books itself, and releases itself on discharge.

Admission · Packages · Room auto-booking
🧾

One-Tap Discharge Billing

Final bill, discount, amount paid and balance due — computed instantly. Print an A5 receipt, record the payment mode, done. Length of stay tracked automatically.

Auto billing · Receipts · Cash/Card/Bank
💰

Dues Worklist

Every unpaid balance in one prioritised list — oldest first, with the patient's mobile number right there for the follow-up call. Click through to record the payment.

Collections · Aging · Quick-open
🔬

Lab & Ultrasound Catalogue

A configurable test catalogue — CBC, LFT, thyroid profile, obstetric scans and more. Multi-select tests on any visit and fees aggregate automatically.

Test fees · Codes · Turnaround time
🏥

Live Hospital Map

Your hospital as visual tiles — every block, floor and room with live occupancy. See who's in which bed, spot free VIP rooms, click a room to open the patient.

Bed management · Occupancy · VIP rooms
🔁

Referral Tracking

When the OPD doctor recommends a lab test, scan or surgery, Obstella remembers. Pending referrals stay visible until completed — so no patient falls through the cracks.

Care pathways · Follow-up compliance
🛡️

Built-In Protection

Secure login with account lockout, password recovery via security question or recovery key — and an automatic backup engine that ships with the app.

  • Auto-backup monthly to up to 3 destinations — local, USB, OneDrive or network
  • Integrity-checked, zipped backups with 30-copy retention per destination
  • One-click restore that preserves your current database as a safety copy
  • 14-day rolling activity logs
⚙️

Your Hospital, Your Rules

Configure everything: doctors and their fees, procedures, surgery packages, visit categories with colours, patient-type pricing and queue token printing.

Master data · Pricing · Tokens

Tech Specs

Engineered to be invisible.

No servers. No cloud bills. No IT department. Obstella installs in minutes and runs for years.

Platform
Windows desktop applicationWindows 7 SP1 through Windows 11 · Inno Setup installer
Architecture
3-layer .NET (Framework 4.8)GHP.Core services · GHP.Data repositories · GHP.UI WinForms
Database
Embedded SQLite, WAL modeSingle-file database — zero administration, years of data in one file
Performance
Pre-aggregated reporting engineIncome summaries maintained by database triggers — daily report <500 ms, yearly <3 s
Privacy
100% offline & on-premisePatient data never leaves the hospital PC
Security
Authenticated accessAccount lockout after 5 failed attempts · security-question / recovery-key reset
Backup
Automatic, multi-destinationMonthly auto-backup to up to 3 locations · integrity-verified · 30-copy retention
Printing
A5 receipts & queue tokensDischarge receipts, visit tokens with per-category daily numbering, PDF reports
Interface
Custom-themed modern UIKPI tiles, charts, colour-coded categories, responsive sidebar
System requirements
Any PC from the last 15 years4 GB RAM is plenty · launches in under 2 seconds
